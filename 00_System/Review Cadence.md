# Review Cadence

## Daily or Job-Day
- Capture field issues, mistakes, or improvements in `00_System/Daily Notes/`.
- Update an SOP or equipment note immediately if the lesson is important enough to affect the next job.

## Weekly
- Review the maintenance dashboard.
- Promote any durable lessons from Daily Notes into permanent notes.
- Clean up incomplete or unclear capture notes before they age into clutter.

## Monthly
- Review high-use equipment for maintenance, repair, and restock needs.
- Check whether any active SOP or checklist is drifting from real field behavior.

## Quarterly
- Review active SOPs and checklists for accuracy.
- Archive stale research, superseded drafts, or retired-equipment detail that no longer belongs in live folders.

## Seasonal
- Review service-specific SOPs before their busy season.
- Confirm seasonal tools, chemicals, and maintenance routines are ready before demand spikes.

## Triggered Reviews
- Review immediately after any equipment failure, near-miss, customer complaint, or process change that reveals a gap in the system.
