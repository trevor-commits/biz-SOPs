# Tree Trimming Removal

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll remove and dispose of tree trimmings so your yard is clear and ready to use again.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $300.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Collect and haul away branches, sticks, and leaf debris
- Light rake or blow-off of the work area
- Dispose of everything properly

## Not Included
- Tree trimming itself (available separately)
- Stump grinding or large trunk removal
- Clearing storm-damaged or fallen whole trees

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Keeps your yard clean and safe after trimming
- Avoids hazards from leftover debris
- Bundles easily with trimming service for a complete job

Pricing depends on the amount of debris and disposal fees.
