# Penetration Sealing / Surface Closure After Removal of Satellite Dishes

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> After the dishes are removed, we’ll seal or close exposed holes and penetrations left at the mounting points. This is basic weather sealing only, using appropriate sealant or patch material based on whether the location is at the roof, eaves, or stucco/wall surface.

## Included
- Seal exposed holes left by removed hardware
- Close basic penetrations at roof, eaves, or wall surfaces
- Apply appropriate sealant or patch material by surface type
- Leave mounting areas in a closed, weather-sealed condition

## Not Included
- Cosmetic repair or surface restoration
- Painting or texture matching
- Shingle replacement or roof repair
- Stucco patch blending or finish work

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps limit moisture entry at former mounting points
- Addresses exposed penetrations as part of the removal work
- Keeps the scope clear and easy to document
