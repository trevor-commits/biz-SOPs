# Leaf Raking

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Tired of staring at leaf piles? We’ll rake them up, load your cans, and leave everything ready for your green waste pickup.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $100.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Rake leaves from lawn, hardscape, and open planting beds
- Pile and load leaves into your yard waste cans (or paper yard bags if provided)
- Light blow-off of walkways and driveway after raking
- Consolidate extra leaf piles neatly if cans fill up
- Do a quick visual walk-through of the work area before we wrap up  ￼

## Not Included
- Hauling leaves or debris offsite
- Gutter cleaning or roof debris removal
- Detailed garden bed weeding or pruning
- Mowing, edging, or other lawn care services
- Disposal fees or extra cans/bags (we use what you provide)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Keeps leaves from smothering your lawn and plants
- Reduces slip hazards on wet patios, steps, and walkways
- Saves you a weekend of raking and lifting heavy cans
- Leaves are ready for your regular trash/green waste service

## Just So You Know
Pricing is based on yard size, access, and how much debris we’re dealing with, charged per visit. Please have your empty yard waste cans or bags ready and accessible when we arrive.
