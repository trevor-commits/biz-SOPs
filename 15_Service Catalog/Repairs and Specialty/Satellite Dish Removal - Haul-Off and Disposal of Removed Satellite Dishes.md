# Haul-Off and Disposal of Removed Satellite Dishes

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We will haul off and dispose of the removed satellite dishes and related hardware as part of this job. This is included because onsite dumpster space is not available.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Load removed dishes and mounting hardware for removal from the property
- Haul off all materials generated by the dish removals
- Dispose of removed materials offsite

## Not Included
- Disposal of unrelated property debris or bulk items
- Use of onsite dumpster space
- Haul-off of materials not generated by this scope

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Keeps removed materials from being left onsite
- Avoids using limited property space for debris storage
- Leaves the work area cleared of the removed dish materials

Haul-off and disposal for the removed satellite dishes and related hardware. This applies only to materials generated from this scope of work.
