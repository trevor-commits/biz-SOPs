# Cobweb Removal

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Removal of visible cobwebs around entryways, eaves, porch ceilings, window frames, and other accessible exterior areas.

> - Extension pole brushing to clear high corners and hard-to-reach spots
> - Light brushing of exterior light fixtures and trim
> - Cleanup of brushed cobweb material from ground-level surfaces

> Note: Service focuses on visible cobwebs in accessible areas.
