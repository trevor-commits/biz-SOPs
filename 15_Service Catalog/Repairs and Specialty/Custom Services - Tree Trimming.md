# Tree Trimming

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Tree Trimming – Cut Back Branches That Cause Problems
>
> Branches that touch or hang over the house can scrape roofs, break gutters, and dump debris. We’ll trim them back around the home.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $50.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Trim small–medium branches away from roofs, gutters, and walkways
- Cut back limbs around solar panels, skylights, and windows
- Light tidy of the immediate work area (blow/rake)

## Not Included
- Large-limb work, climbing, or crane jobs
- Full debris haul-away (see Tree Trimming Removal)
- Stump grinding or full tree removal

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps prevent roof and gutter damage
- Reduces leaf/needle buildup and clogs
- Keeps entries and paths clear

This is light–moderate trimming from ground or ladder. Pricing depends on tree size, access, and how much we cut.
