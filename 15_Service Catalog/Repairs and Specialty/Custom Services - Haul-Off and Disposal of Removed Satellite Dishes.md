# Haul-Off and Disposal of Removed Satellite Dishes

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll remove the discarded satellite dishes from the property and dispose of them off site. This is included for site cleanup and convenience since the customer does not have room in the onsite dumpster.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Load removed dishes and hardware from the property
- Haul debris off site
- Dispose of removed materials
- Leave the work area cleared of removed dish debris

## Not Included
- Disposal of unrelated jobsite debris
- Dumpster service or onsite container rental
- Removal of equipment not included in this scope

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Keeps debris out of the property’s dumpster
- Simplifies cleanup for management and maintenance staff
- Completes the job without leaving removed materials behind

Included at no additional charge. This applies to the 9 removed satellite dishes and their associated mounting hardware only.
