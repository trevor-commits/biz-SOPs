# Satellite Dish Removal – Two-Story Apartment Buildings

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll remove satellite dishes from two-story apartment buildings and take down the attached mounting hardware at each location. This covers removal of 9 dish assemblies as whole units from roof, eaves, and wall-mounted locations as applicable.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Access two-story mounting locations
- Detach each dish as a whole unit
- Remove associated brackets and mounting hardware
- Lower and stage removed units for haul-off

## Not Included
- Disassembly of dishes into parts on site
- Roofing, carpentry, or stucco repair
- Painting, texture matching, or finish work
- New equipment installation or relocation

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Clears obsolete equipment from the buildings
- Includes two-story access as part of the scope
- Removes attached hardware, not just the dish face
- Leaves mounting areas ready for basic closure

Priced per dish.
