# Roof Leak Diagnosis – $50/hour

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll track down the source of a roof leak with a focused inspection and targeted water testing so you know what’s going on and how to fix it.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $50.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Inspect roof surfaces, tiles/shingles, flashing, penetrations, and valleys
- Check inside (attic/ceiling areas) if safely accessible
- Controlled hose testing to replicate the leak when appropriate
- Document findings with notes/photos and recommend repairs

## Not Included
- Permanent repairs or materials
- Interior remediation (drywall/paint/flooring)
- Opening walls/ceilings or any destructive testing

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Pinpoints the actual entry point (not just the stain you see inside)
- Helps you avoid repeat leaks and guesswork repairs
- Gives you a clear plan and estimate for the fix

Rate is $50/hour with a 1-hour minimum. Weather and roof safety may limit testing on the day of service. If water testing is used, minor dripping can occur by design to confirm the source.
