# Under Roof Tile Cleaning - Bird Droppings

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll remove the buildup from accessible under-tile areas and disinfect so the space is safer and cleaner.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $100.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Set up ladder access and wear protective PPE
- Remove droppings and nesting debris from accessible under-tile/eave areas
- Bag and dispose of waste on site
- Apply disinfectant to treated areas
- Clean up any fallout around the work area

## Not Included
- Lifting/removing roof tiles to reach hidden areas
- Repairs to tiles, underlayment, fascia, vents, or soffits
- Full roof cleaning or moss/algae treatment
- Bird proofing/mesh installation
- Attic cleanup or insulation removal

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces bacteria/parasite exposure around your home
- Helps limit staining and corrosion on nearby materials
- Cuts down odor and buildup that attracts more activity

## Just So You Know
Priced per visit or hourly (quote provided based on access and how much waste is present).
