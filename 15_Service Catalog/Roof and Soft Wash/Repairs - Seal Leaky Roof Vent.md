# Seal Leaky Roof Vent

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> A small leak around a roof vent can turn into ceiling stains and drywall damage. We’ll seal the vent so water sheds the way it should.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $80.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Inspect the vent boot, flashing, and shingles around the leaking vent
- Clean the area so sealant can bond properly
- Reseal exposed nail heads, gaps, and cracked sealant at the vent
- Apply roof-grade sealant or flashing tape where appropriate
- Take photos before/after on request so you can see what was done

## Not Included
- Full roof inspection or full-roof leak tracing
- Replacement of the vent, boot, or surrounding shingles
- Repairs to interior damage (paint, drywall, insulation, framing)
- Work on other roof penetrations (chimneys, skylights, pipes) unless added to the job
- Long-term “no leak anywhere on the roof” guarantee—this targets the vent only

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps stop active drips and ceiling stains from getting worse
- Buys time before larger, more expensive roof work is needed
- Keeps wind-driven rain from sneaking in around the vent
- Gives you a simple, documented fix you can share with a roofer or insurance if needed

Pricing is typically per vent, with additional time billed at our hourly diagnostic rate if the leak source is more complex than a simple vent seal
