# Plastic Bird Spike Installation

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll install plastic bird spikes on your roofline, gutters, and ledges to prevent birds from roosting and nesting in unwanted areas.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $16.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Install plastic bird spikes along key roosting spots (roofline, gutters, ledges)
- Secure the spikes with adhesive or screws, ensuring they’re firmly in place
- Clean the surface and remove any existing debris before installation
- Use a safe, non-toxic adhesive and apply as needed

## Not Included
- Full roof cleaning or repairs
- Additional birdproofing measures (e.g., netting or solar panel mesh)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Affordable option compared to metal spikes
- Quick and easy installation with minimal disruption
- Helps prevent bird nesting and roosting in problem areas
- Low-maintenance once installed

Plastic bird spikes are cheaper, but they don’t last as long as metal ones and may become yellowish or brittle within 1–2 years depending on weather exposure. Annual maintenance can extend their life.
