# Metal Bird Spike Installation

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll install high-quality metal bird spikes to protect your roofline, gutters, and ledges from unwanted bird activity, providing long-lasting and effective deterrence.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $20.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Install metal bird spikes along key roosting spots (roofline, gutters, ledges)
- Secure the spikes with screws or appropriate fasteners
- Clean the surface and remove any existing debris before installation
- Use weather-resistant materials for maximum durability

## Not Included
- Full roof cleaning or repairs
- Additional birdproofing measures (e.g., netting or solar panel mesh)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Long-lasting and durable, even in harsh weather conditions
- Effective at preventing bird nesting without needing frequent maintenance
- Provides a permanent solution for bird control
- Sleek, professional look that won’t detract from your home’s appearance

Metal spikes are more expensive but last longer than plastic alternatives. They are ideal for high-impact areas and will stand up to the elements without becoming discolored or brittle.
