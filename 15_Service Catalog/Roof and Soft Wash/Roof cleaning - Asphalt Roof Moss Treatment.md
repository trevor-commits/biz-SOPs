# Asphalt Roof Moss Treatment

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Treat moss growth on asphalt shingle roofing using a low-pressure roof-safe application. This service is for asphalt roofs only and is intended to address organic growth without using high-pressure washing.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Inspect the roof surface for visible moss growth and treatment needs
- Apply moss treatment to affected asphalt shingle areas
- Use a low-pressure application method suited for asphalt roofing
- Treat organic growth with the goal of stopping further moss activity
- Note visible roof condition issues that may affect service results

## Not Included
- Manual scraping, brushing, or physical moss removal unless listed separately
- Gutter cleaning or removal of roof debris from gutters and downspouts
- Roof repairs, shingle replacement, or leak diagnosis
- Treatment of tile, metal, cedar, or other non-asphalt roofing
- Cleanup of heavy moss falloff from landscaping or surrounding areas unless listed separately

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Slows ongoing moss growth that can hold moisture against the roof
- Helps reduce further buildup in shaded or damp areas
- Lowers the need for harsher cleaning methods on asphalt shingles
- Supports ongoing roof maintenance between larger services or repairs

Pricing is typically based on roof size, moss severity, access, and how much of the roof needs treatment. Moss usually dies first and releases gradually over time, so the roof may not look fully cleared immediately after service.
