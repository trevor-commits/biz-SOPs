# Roof Tile Repair/Replacement

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If you’ve got cracked, chipped, or missing roof tiles, we’ll fix the problem before it leads to leaks or water damage. Whether we can repair it or need to replace it, we’ll handle it with care and attention to detail.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $50.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Inspect the damaged area and check nearby tiles for impact or movement
- Remove broken tiles carefully without disturbing the surrounding area
- Repair minor cracks using appropriate sealants and tools
- Replace tiles when they’re beyond repair using best-matching materials
- Confirm proper fit and alignment to maintain water flow

## Not Included
- Replacement of large roof sections
- Repairs of the actual roof or material underneath tile
- Full roof inspection or leak tracing beyond the repair area
- Cleaning underneath tiles

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Prevents roof leaks and underlayment damage
- Helps avoid costly ceiling stains and interior water issues
- Keeps your roof looking uniform and functioning properly
- Extends the life of your tile roof by staying ahead of small problems

If matching tiles are unavailable, we’ll source the closest possible option — or use one you provide. Some older tile types may be discontinued, but we’ll always let you know your options before starting.
Pricing depends on roof height, access, and number of tiles being repaired or replaced.
