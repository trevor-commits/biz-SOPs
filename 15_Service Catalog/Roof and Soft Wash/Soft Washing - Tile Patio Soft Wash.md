# Tile Patio Soft Wash

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Soft washing is a gentle, chemical-assisted cleaning method that’s ideal for tile patios where pressure washing could damage the surface or grout. Great for removing organic buildup like algae, moss, and grime without using high pressure.
>
> Includes:
> - Application of a tile-safe cleaning solution to loosen dirt and organic growth
> - Light brushing or agitation as needed
> - Low-pressure rinse to wash away buildup without damaging tile or grout
> - Careful attention to edges, grout lines, and shaded areas where buildup is common
>
> Benefits:
> - Safely removes moss, algae, and grime without damaging delicate surfaces
> - Restores tile appearance and helps prevent slippery areas
> - Preserves grout lines and protects against cracking from aggressive cleaning
> - Ideal for more fragile or decorative tile surfaces that can’t handle high pressure
>
> Note:
> This is a low-pressure process — not a pressure wash. Results depend on tile condition, buildup type, and grout age.
> Pricing depends on square footage and condition of the patio.
