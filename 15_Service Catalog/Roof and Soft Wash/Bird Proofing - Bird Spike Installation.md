# Bird Spike Installation

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Once the area is cleaned, we’ll install deterrents such as bird spikes in the problem zones — ledges, rooflines, and vents — to keep birds from nesting or roosting again.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Inspect the property to find common roosting/nesting spots
- Install bird spikes or other deterrents where needed
- Secure materials without damaging roofing, gutters, or siding
- Test placement to ensure coverage in problem areas

## Not Included
- Full exclusion netting (requires specialized service)
- Pest control for other animals (rodents, insects, etc.)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Stops birds from landing and nesting on your property
- Protects against droppings, feathers, and nesting messes returning
- Helps protect your family’s health by reducing exposure to bird waste
- Long-term solution with minimal upkeep

## Just So You Know
Pricing is based on linear footage, access, and type of deterrent installed. We’ll recommend the best approach for your property after inspection.
