# Roof Treatment Courtesy Follow-Up

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Return after service to check treatment progress and confirm the roof is responding as expected. This visit is a visual follow-up for previously treated roof areas.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Reinspect previously treated roof areas from the ground or accessible vantage points
- Check visible moss condition and treatment progress
- Look for areas where moss remains heavy or progress appears uneven
- Document visible results with notes or photos when needed
- Recommend next steps if additional treatment or related service appears needed

## Not Included
- New roof treatment beyond the original approved scope
- Roof repairs, leak diagnosis, or shingle replacement
- Full roof cleaning or removal of newly fallen debris
- Gutter cleaning or cleanup of roof runoff debris
- Walking fragile or unsafe roof sections unless separately approved

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps confirm the treatment is progressing normally
- Catches areas that may need more attention before they become a bigger issue
- Gives the customer a clear update after enough time has passed
- Creates a cleaner handoff between the original service and any next recommendation

Pricing is per follow-up visit unless included with the original service. This is a visual courtesy check about one month after treatment, not a guarantee that all moss will be fully gone by that date.**
