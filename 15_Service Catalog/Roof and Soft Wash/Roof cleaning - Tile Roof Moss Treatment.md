# Tile Roof Moss Treatment

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll apply a tile-safe moss killer that treats moss, algae, and lichen without scraping or pressure washing. The growth dies and weathers off over time.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.55
> - Unit: ft²
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price and unit are present.

## Included
- Light blow-off of loose roof debris (if accessible)
- Apply moss treatment at the proper strength for tile
- Focus on shaded valleys, north sides, and heavy growth areas
- No scraping or pressure washing of tile
- Flush the gutters of all debris
- Rinse plants to make sure no overspray contaminates them

## Not Included
- Rinse-off/physical removal in the same visit
- Gutter cleaning or repairs
- Roof tile/underlayment repairs

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps prevent moisture from sitting under moss and causing leaks
- Lowers fire risk by eliminating dry moss over time
- Helps keep gutters from clogging with moss chunks
- Supports insurance requirements to remove visible roof moss

Priced per square foot of roof area. Moss browns in 1–3 weeks and sheds gradually over several weeks/months. Environmentally compliant application; runoff is managed.
