# Roof Bird Waste Cleaning and Removal

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Bird droppings are unsightly, corrosive, and can carry harmful bacteria. We’ll safely remove and disinfect the area so it’s clean and safe again.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00

## Included
- Remove droppings, feathers, and nesting debris
- Clean and disinfect surfaces to neutralize stains and germs
- Dispose of waste properly

## Not Included
- Repairs to roofing, siding, or wiring
- Installation of bird spikes or other deterrents (available separately)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Protects your property from damage and staining
- Reduces health risks from bacteria and parasites
