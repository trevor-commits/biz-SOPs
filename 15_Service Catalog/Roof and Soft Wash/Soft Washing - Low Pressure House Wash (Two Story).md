# Low Pressure House Wash (Two Story)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Dirt, algae, mildew, and cobwebs build up on siding over time, making your home look dull and worn. We use a cleaning solution with sodium hypochlorite (the active ingredient in bleach), but only at the strength needed to clean effectively without damaging siding, paint, or other surfaces.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Apply a sodium hypochlorite-based cleaning solution at the proper strength for your home
- Rinse thoroughly with low-pressure water (safe for siding and paint)
- Brush or rinse away cobwebs around siding, trim, and eaves
- Protect keyholes, outlets, and other sensitive areas to avoid damage
- Take special care around plants and landscaping — let us know if you have delicate plants so we can cover, rinse, or move them if needed

## Not Included
- Roof washing, gutter cleaning, or window cleaning (available separately)
- Paint or stain removal
- Cleaning of delicate surfaces not safe for washing
- Oxidation Removal

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Restores your home’s curb appeal quickly
- Removes algae, mildew, grime, and cobwebs that make siding look neglected
- Cleans effectively while protecting siding, paint, and surroundings
- Helps extend the life of your siding by keeping it maintained

Please make sure all windows are closed before your house wash. If window seals are not in good condition, cleaning solution may get inside or between panes.
Pricing depends on square footage, siding type, and level of buildup.
