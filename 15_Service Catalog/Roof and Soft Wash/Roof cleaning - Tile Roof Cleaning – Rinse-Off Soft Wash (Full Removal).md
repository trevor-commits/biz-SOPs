# Tile Roof Cleaning – Rinse-Off Soft Wash (Full Removal)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Full-service tile roof cleaning using a professional soft-wash treatment followed by a controlled low-pressure rinse. Ideal for heavier growth or customers wanting immediate visual results.
>
> Includes:
> - Brushing or blowing of loose debris and heavy moss (if accessible)
> - Application of roof-safe cleaning solution to kill moss, algae, and lichen
> - Full rinse using low-pressure water — safe for clay and concrete tile
> - Perimeter cleanup of all roof runoff and debris
> - Visual inspection of roof for damage, loose tiles, or water entry points
> - Optional before/after photo documentation
>
> Benefits:
> - Removes growth more quickly and restores roof appearance immediately
> - Prevents leaks, rot, fire risk, and drainage issues
> - Safe for tile — no high pressure or scraping
> - Meets insurance and environmental compliance standards
> - Higher-level service ideal for homes with heavy buildup or resale prep
>
> Note:
> We avoid scraping or pressure washing tile to prevent removal of protective coating of roof or directly damage it. This service involves careful rinsing using soft-wash tools. Not recommended for highly brittle or deteriorating tile roofs.
> Pricing is based on total roof square footage and is higher than leave-on treatment due to additional labor and cleanup.
