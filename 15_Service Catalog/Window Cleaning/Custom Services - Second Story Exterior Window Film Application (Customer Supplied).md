# Second Story Exterior Window Film Application (Customer Supplied)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll install your exterior-rated reflective film on a second-story window from the outside with a trim gap and a proper silicone edge seal.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $200.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Prep and clean the exterior glass to film-ready condition
- Wet-apply your film, align, and precision-trim (1–2 mm perimeter gap)
- Hard-squeegee and detail edges; leave written cure/aftercare
- Return in 24–72 hours to apply a neutral-cure silicone edge seal once the perimeter is dry

## Not Included
- Interior window cleaning
- Removal of old film/adhesive, hard-water stain removal, or scratch repair
- Interior-only/“flip” installs of exterior-rated film
- Textured/wired/specialty-coated glass, skylights, or panes needing special rigging beyond ladder/roof access

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Cuts glare and solar heat before it reaches the glass
- Daytime privacy with a uniform mirror look from outside
- UV reduction to help minimize interior fading

- Curing look: New film can appear hazy/bubbly while moisture evaporates—expect 1–3 weeks depending on weather. No cleaning for 30 days.
- Privacy: Reflective films provide daytime privacy only; at night (lights on) privacy reverses.
- Keep it dry: After we seal, keep water off the seal for 24 hours; avoid washing/sprinklers for 72 hours. Full cure is typically 24–48 hours.
- Do not open the window for at least 24 hours after the edge seal is applied.
- Sprinklers: If wind can blow sprinkler mist to this window, turn them off for 72 hours after sealing.
- Thermal stress: Dark/reflective films raise glass temperature; on some dual-pane/low-E units with shade lines or edge chips, crack risk increases.
- Weather: Exterior installs require calm, dry conditions; wind or rain may require rescheduling.
- Warranty: Customer-supplied material = labor-only workmanship warranty (no warranty on the film, glass, or seals).

Prep before we arrive:
- Confirm the film is exterior-rated and not creased
- Clear ground area below the window; move vehicles/outdoor items; secure pets/kids

Aftercare basics:
- Don’t touch film edges; let midday sun help curing
- First clean after 30 days with a soft cloth and a non-ammonia cleaner (optional)
