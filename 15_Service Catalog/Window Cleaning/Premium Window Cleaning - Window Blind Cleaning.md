# Window Blind Cleaning

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll gently clean your window blinds to remove dust, pet hair, and buildup so they look fresher and function better. It’s a great add-on for spring cleaning or after a remodel.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Wipe down each slat by hand using a microfiber cloth or duster
- Lightly vacuum blinds if there’s heavy dust or pet hair buildup
- Spot clean edges, cords, and brackets as needed
- Handle gently to avoid bending or damaging delicate materials

## Not Included
- Deep cleaning or soaking of fabric or heavily stained blinds
- Removal and reinstallation of blinds
- Repairs to cords, slats, or mounting hardware

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Gets rid of the dust that settles and sticks to blinds over time
- Helps improve air quality and reduce allergens
- Makes the whole room feel cleaner without replacing the blinds
- Perfect finishing touch when you're already cleaning windows

Not all blinds can be cleaned the same way. We’ll take a look first and let you know what’s safe and realistic. Pricing depends on how many blinds you have and how dusty or delicate they are.
