# Window Screen Mesh Replacement – Premium High Visibility Mesh (UltraVue)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Your screens take a beating from sun, pets, and everyday wear. We’ll replace the torn or brittle mesh with UltraVue high-visibility material so you get airflow and a clearer view.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Remove the screen frame (as needed) and take out the old mesh and spline
- Clean the screen channel and prep the frame for re-meshing
- Install UltraVue high-visibility mesh and new spline for a tight fit
- Reinstall the screen and verify it sits properly in the window

## Not Included
- Frame rebuilding, bent frame straightening, or corner/hardware replacement
- Pet-proof mesh upgrades or specialty security screens
- Window track cleaning or window washing
- Repairs to window latches, rollers, or window operation issues
- New screen frame fabrication (full new screens)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Improves visibility without sacrificing airflow
- Helps keep bugs out with a tighter, newer mesh
- Extends the life of your existing screen frames

Priced per screen, based on screen size and frame condition.
If the frame is warped or damaged, the mesh may not tension evenly and we’ll let you know what options make sense.
