# Sliding Screen Door Roller Restoration

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We restore the rolling hardware on the sliding screen door by cleaning up the wheel and bolt assembly and reinstalling it. This includes rust removal, wire brushing, and greasing the wheels before putting them back in place.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $95.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Remove the wheel and bolt hardware
- Soak rusted parts in rust remover
- Wire brush the wheels and bolts
- Reinstall the wheels
- Grease the wheels after reinstalling

## Not Included
- New wheels, bolts, or other replacement parts
- Screen mesh repair or replacement
- Frame repair or track repair
- Lock, handle, or latch repair
- Full door replacement or new door installation

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Improves appearance and functionality of existing hardware
- Helps the door roll more smoothly
- Clarifies that this is restoration work, not full replacement

Pricing is typically based per door unless noted otherwise. This is restoration of the existing wheel hardware, so final results depend on the condition of the original parts.
