# Sliding Screen Door Frame Painting - One side

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We paint one side of the sliding screen door frame. This is for the frame only and does not include the mesh or full door restoration.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $120.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Prep the paintable side of the screen door frame
- Apply paint to one side of the frame only
- Work around the existing screen and frame layout
- Clean up the immediate work area when finished

## Not Included
- Painting both sides of the screen door frame
- Screen mesh repair or replacement
- Track cleaning or sliding door service
- Dent repair, frame straightening, or hardware replacement

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Freshens up the side of the door you see most
- Helps the frame look more uniform with nearby trim or doors
- Covers worn or faded finish on that side
- Keeps the scope focused if only one side needs attention

## Just So You Know
Pricing is per door side unless noted otherwise. Color match and finish can vary slightly depending on the existing condition and surface.
