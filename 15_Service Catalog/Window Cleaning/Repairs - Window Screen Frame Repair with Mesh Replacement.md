# Window Screen Frame Repair w/ Mesh Replacement

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We repair damaged window screen frames and replace worn or torn mesh so the screen fits properly and does its job again. This is a good option when the frame is bent, separated, or loose and the mesh also needs to be redone.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Inspect the frame, corners, and screen fit
- Re-square or rebuild the frame as needed
- Replace damaged frame sections or hardware when applicable
- Install new screen mesh and spline if necessary
- Reinstall the screen and check fit in the opening

## Not Included
- Window cleaning
- Pet-resistant or specialty mesh unless added separately

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps the screen sit properly in the window
- Reduces gaps that let bugs and debris in
- Replaces torn or stretched mesh
- Extends the life of the screen when the frame can still be repaired

## Just So You Know
Priced per screen. Final price depends on screen size, frame condition, mesh type, and whether standard repair materials will match the existing frame.
