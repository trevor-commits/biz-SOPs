# Large Sliding Screen Door Mesh Replacement – Premium Pet Resistant Mesh

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If your sliding screen door has tears, holes, or loose mesh, we’ll re-screen it with premium pet-resistant material so it holds up better to claws and daily use.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Remove the old screen mesh and spline
- Inspect the frame and screen channel for fit issues
- Install premium pet-resistant mesh and new spline as needed
- Tension and trim the mesh for a clean, tight finish
- Rehang the screen door and verify it rolls and latches properly

## Not Included
- Frame replacement or structural door repairs
- Roller, latch, or track repairs/replacement
- Track cleaning or lubrication
- Custom color-matching beyond available mesh options
- Full door adjustment if the slider is warped or out of square

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Stops bugs from getting in through rips and gaps
- Holds up better against pets than standard fiberglass mesh
- Extends the life of your existing screen door frame
- Improves door glide by correcting loose or wavy screening

Priced per screen door.
Final price depends on door size, frame condition, and how much spline/channel cleanup is needed.
