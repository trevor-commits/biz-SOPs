# Window Blind Removal

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Careful removal of window blinds and associated hardware from window frames.
>
> Includes:
> - Detaching of blinds from mounting brackets
> - Removal of screws, anchors, and brackets from window trim or drywall
> - Light cleanup of dust or debris from mounting area
>
> Note: Does not include patching, painting, or disposal of blinds or hardware. Blinds must be accessible and free of excessive obstruction.
