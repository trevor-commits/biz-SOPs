# Residential Window Tint Installation - Customer provided film

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We install window film on your home’s glass and come back on a second day to seal the edges after the film cures.  ￼

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Clean and prep the glass before installation
- Apply residential window film to the agreed windows
- Trim and set the film to fit each pane
- Return after drying to seal the film edges

## Not Included
- Window film
- Glass repair or scratch removal
- Removal of old film unless added separately
- Frame, track, or screen cleaning
- Film replacement for failed seals, broken glass, or moisture between panes

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces glare and helps with daytime privacy
- Helps lower heat and sun exposure through the glass
- Gives the film a cleaner, more secure finished edge
- Includes a follow-up visit so final sealing is done after curing

Priced per window. Some light haze or small water pockets can show during drying, and the return visit for edge sealing is done after the film has had time to cure.
