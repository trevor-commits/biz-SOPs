# Second Story Window Paint Removal

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll remove paint and other stubborn construction-type debris from the exterior glass of this second-story window by hand using safe, professional techniques. Because of the height, ladder access, and extra detailing involved, this service takes more time and care than standard window cleaning.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Carefully remove paint, overspray, and stuck-on debris from exterior glass
- Use professional hand tools and techniques designed for glass-safe detailing
- Work through problem areas by hand for a cleaner, more uniform finish
- Access the window safely using ladder setup and careful positioning as needed

## Not Included
- Hard-water stain removal
- Scratched, etched, or permanently damaged glass
- Interior paint or debris removal
- Full post-construction cleanup beyond the quoted window area

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Restores clarity to glass affected by paint or construction debris
- Improves the appearance of the window beyond standard cleaning alone
- Helps remove stubborn buildup that regular window cleaning does not address
- Gives hard-to-reach second-story glass the extra detail it needs

## Just So You Know
Pricing depends on the amount of paint or debris present, the time required for removal, and second-story access.
This service is priced separately from standard window cleaning because it requires more detailed labor and specialty removal work.
