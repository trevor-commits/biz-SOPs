# Window & Door Lubrication

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll lubricate the tracks, hinges, and moving parts of your windows and sliding doors to help them open and close more easily. It’s a quick service that makes a big difference in day-to-day use.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Apply silicone-based lubricant to window tracks and sliding door rails
- Treat hinges, rollers, and other moving parts where accessible
- Wipe away excess buildup or dust that may be causing drag
- Check for signs of sticking, squeaking, or uneven movement

## Not Included
- Repairs to broken parts, locks, or rollers
- Deep cleaning of tracks or debris removal (available separately)
- Service on items that are sealed shut or non-functional

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Makes windows and doors easier to operate
- Reduces wear and tear on rollers, hinges, and tracks
- Helps prevent sticking or jamming, especially in older frames
- Quick, affordable maintenance that extends the life of your hardware

This service pairs well with interior window cleaning or screen service. Pricing depends on how many windows or doors need attention and accessibility.
