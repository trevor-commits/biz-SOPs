# Normal Window Sized Screen Replacement (Premium Vue Mesh)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If your window screens are torn, brittle, or no longer doing their job, we can replace just the mesh without needing to replace the whole frame. Choose from a range of mesh types to match your needs and budget.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $100.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Remove the damaged screen from the frame
- Replace with new mesh, cut and fitted to size
- Secure with new spline and reinstall in the existing frame
- Check for a clean, tight fit before returning the screen to you

## Not Included
- Frame repairs or replacement (this service covers mesh only)
- Screens that are bent, warped, or damaged beyond repair

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Restores screen function without replacing the frame
- Keeps bugs and debris out while allowing airflow
- Helps your windows look clean and maintained
- Choose from standard, pet-resistant, or solar mesh options

We install several mesh types including standard, pet-resistant, and solar screen. Let us know what you're looking for and we’ll help you pick the right fit.

Pricing depends on mesh type, screen size, and how many screens you need done.
