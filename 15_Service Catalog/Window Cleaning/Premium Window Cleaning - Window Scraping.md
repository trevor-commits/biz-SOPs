# Window Scraping

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If you’ve got regular windows with things like old paint drips, tape, tree sap, or sticker residue — we can carefully scrape it off to get your glass clean again. This is not a full construction clean, just targeted scraping where needed.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Use scrapers or blades to remove stuck-on debris without damaging the glass
- Target specific problem areas like paint specks, tape, sap, or sticker glue
- Clean the glass afterward for a clear, even finish
- Let you know if anything looks risky or requires extra time

## Not Included
- Full post-construction window cleaning
- Hard-water stain removal or deep glass restoration
- Repairs for scratched or damaged glass

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Removes stubborn spots regular cleaning can’t touch
- Helps restore clarity to neglected or mistreated windows
- Gives your windows a noticeably cleaner, more finished look

## Just So You Know
Scraping is only done when it's safe for the glass type. Some tempered or low-E coated windows are more prone to scratching — if there’s any risk, we’ll let you know before starting. Pricing depends on how many windows need scraping and how much buildup there is.
