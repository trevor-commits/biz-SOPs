# Drip Edge Replacement – Standard Section

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If you’ve got a smaller problem area, we can install drip edge on that short run so water sheds into the gutter instead of behind it.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Prep the problem area and remove/loosen fasteners as needed
- Install new drip edge on a standard section (up to about 10')
- Secure with standard materials and hardware
- Clean up jobsite debris

## Not Included
- Any gutter leveling, reshaping, or re-hanging
- Full gutter cleaning or downspout flushing
- Fascia/roof deck repairs or repainting
- Replacing gutters or downspouts

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces water getting behind the gutter at the problem spot
- Helps keep runoff going into the gutter at that section
- Prevents small issues from spreading into a larger section

Priced per standard section. Best-match materials are used, but exact color/finish matches can vary by manufacturer and age.
