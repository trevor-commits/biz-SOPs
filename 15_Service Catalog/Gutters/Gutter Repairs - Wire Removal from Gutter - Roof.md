# Wire Removal from Gutter/Roof

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Loose leftover wiring can end up sitting in the gutter line or sticking out along the roof edge. We access the roofline and remove the abandoned wire so it’s out of the way.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Set up ladder access to reach the gutter/roof edge area
- Cut and remove abandoned exterior wire (ex: old satellite/low-voltage line)
- Pull wire out of the gutter line and remove loose clips/straps when possible
- Bag/dispose of removed material and clean up

## Not Included
- Electrical work, testing, or reconnecting services
- Tracing where the wire came from or what it powered
- Removing active utility/provider lines
- Repairs to roofing, fascia, or gutter components

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Keeps wire from catching debris in the gutter line
- Reduces the chance of the wire rubbing or snagging in wind
- Cleans up the roof edge and gutter area

Priced per removal item. We remove what’s accessible and clearly abandoned; anything tied into active equipment is excluded.
