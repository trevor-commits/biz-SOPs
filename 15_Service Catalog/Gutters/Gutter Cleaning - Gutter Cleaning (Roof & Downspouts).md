# Gutter Cleaning (Roof & Downspouts)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll clear leaves, pine needles, and roof debris from your gutters and downspouts so rainwater drains away from your home properly. This does not include patio covers.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.12
> - Unit: Sq. Ft.
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price and unit are present.

## Included
- Remove all gutter debris by hand or blower
- Flush gutters and downspouts (as needed) to confirm flow
- Quick visual check for obvious clogs or disconnected sections
- Clean up the work area before we leave
- Take before/after pictures of the roof and gutters

## Not Included
- Cleaning the outside faces of gutters (separate service)
- Repairs or gutter guard installation
- Patio cover gutter cleaning
- Window cleaning if windows get dirty in the process

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Prevents overflow that can damage fascia, siding, and foundations
- Reduces the chance of roof leaks during heavy rain
- Stays ahead of costly repairs by removing buildup
- Prevents damage to gutters themselves
- Keeps water flowing
- You are able to see a part of your home that you wouldn’t otherwise

Gutter cleaning can get messy and nearby windows may get splashed—we always clean up after ourselves, but window cleaning isn’t included. Pricing typically depends on sq ft of building
