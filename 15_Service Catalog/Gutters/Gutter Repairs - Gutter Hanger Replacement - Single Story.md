# Gutter Hanger Replacement - Single Story

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We replace failed or missing gutter hangers to secure the gutter back to the fascia.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $50.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Remove and dispose of damaged/loose hangers
- Install new hidden hangers where needed
- Re-secure the gutter run to the fascia in the worked area
- Tighten and stabilize sections that have shifted

## Not Included
- Gutter cleaning or downspout flushing
- Outside faces of gutters
- Seam sealing or leak repairs
- Downspout replacement or reroutes
- Gutter guard install or removal

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces sagging that can lead to overflow
- Helps prevent sections from pulling away in wind and rain
- Lowers the chance of joints opening up from movement
