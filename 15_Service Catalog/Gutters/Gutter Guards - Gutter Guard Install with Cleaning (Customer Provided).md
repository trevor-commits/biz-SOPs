# Gutter Guard Install w/Cleaning (Customer Provided)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We will install the guards you provided after cleaning your gutters

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Remove gutter debris by hand or blower
- Flush gutters and downspouts as needed to confirm flow
- Inspect the provided guards for obvious fit issues before starting
- Prep the gutter edge so the guards sit flat
- Cut and install guards to match gutter runs and corners
- Secure the guards based on their design and your roof/gutter edge

## Not Included
- Removal of existing/old guards
- Gutter repairs, seam sealing, or re-leveling
- Cleaning the outside faces of gutters
- Replacing missing/incorrect guard parts or hardware

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Clears existing buildup so water flows correctly right away
- Helps reduce how often gutters clog up moving forward
- Lowers overflow risk during heavy rain

Pricing is per linear foot (LF). Customer-provided guards must be complete (enough pieces, fasteners, and correct size). If the product doesn’t fit or parts are missing, we’ll stop and let you know before going further.
