# Extensive Gutter Cleaning w/Guards

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If your gutters are protected but still backing up or just very dirty, the guards will need to come off so we can clear everything underneath. We’ll remove the existing guards, clean the gutters properly, then reinstall the guards when we’re done.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Remove existing gutter guards (as needed for access)
- Clear debris from inside the gutters by hand/blower
- Flush gutters and downspouts as needed to confirm flow
- Reinstall the existing guards and secure them back in place
- Bag and haul off loosened debris from the work area

## Not Included
- Cleaning the outside faces of gutters
- Repairing broken guards, clips, fasteners, or damaged gutter sections
- Replacing guards or upgrading to a new guard system
- Roof debris removal beyond what falls during guard removal
- Window cleaning (nearby windows may get splashed during flushing)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Stops overflow caused by buildup under the guards
- Helps gutters and downspouts drain the way they should
- Reduces leak risk at seams and corners from standing water
- Extends the time between cleanings on guarded systems

## Just So You Know
Priced per linear foot, with additional labor when guards must be fully removed and reinstalled. Guard removal/reinstall time depends on guard type, fastener condition, and roofline access.
