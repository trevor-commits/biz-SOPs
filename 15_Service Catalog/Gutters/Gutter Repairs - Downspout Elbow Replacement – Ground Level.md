# Downspout Elbow Replacement – Ground Level

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> A cracked or smashed elbow can dump water where you don’t want it. We replace the elbow, trim for fit, and paint it to blend with the downspout.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $60.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Remove the damaged downspout elbow
- Trim/cut for proper fit as needed
- Install a new plastic elbow (customer-provided or approved equivalent)
- Prep and paint the elbow to match the existing downspout

## Not Included
- Replacing vertical downspout runs or gutter outlets
- Underground drain work or extensions beyond the elbow
- Repainting the full downspout or gutter system
- Repairs to landscaping or concrete from previous drainage issues

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Directs water away more predictably at the base
- Reduces leaks and splashing at the elbow connection
- Improves appearance with paint to blend

## Just So You Know
Priced per elbow. Paint is best-match and may look slightly different until it fully cures and weathers.
