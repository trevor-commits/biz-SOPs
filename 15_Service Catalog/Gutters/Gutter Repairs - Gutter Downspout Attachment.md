# Gutter Downspout Attachment

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll attach or reattach loose downspouts so water flows down the pipe like it should — not out the top or sides.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $75.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes

## Included
- Inspect downspout straps, screws, and connection points
- Replace or tighten hardware as needed
- Ensure proper fit and alignment with the gutter outlet

## Not Included
