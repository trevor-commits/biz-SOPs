# Gutter Hanger Repair

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll reinforce your existing gutter hangers so your system stays tight and drains properly.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $10.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Refasten loose hangers with proper screws
- Replace old gutter nails with durable screws

## Not Included
- Installing new hangers (available separately)
- Repairing or replacing damaged fascia

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Stops gutters from sagging or pulling away
- Keeps water flowing where it should
- Extends the life of your current hangers without full replacement

We’ll let you know if any hangers are too worn to be reused.
