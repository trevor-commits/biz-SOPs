# 1-Year Gutter Guard Inspection & Maintenance – Complimentary with Installation

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Every gutter guard system needs a checkup to make sure it’s working the way it should. With every guard installation, your first annual inspection and maintenance visit is included at no extra cost.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Inspect guards for sagging, shifting, or damage
- Blow off roof and clear debris sitting on top of the guards
- Confirm water flow at downspouts

## Not Included
- Full gutter cleaning under the guards
- Repairs or replacement of damaged sections beyond minor adjustments
- Service for systems we didn’t install

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Keeps your gutter guards performing like they should
- Catches small issues before they turn into clogs or overflows
- Extends the life of your gutter system and protects your home
- Gives you peace of mind heading into the rainy season

This service is complimentary for the first year after your guard installation. After that, we recommend yearly maintenance to keep everything flowing right.
