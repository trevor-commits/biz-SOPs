# Gutter Stick Installation

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Gutter Sticks are a low-cost, high-impact way to prevent gutter clogs right at the downspout. We’ll install them where they’re needed most to help keep water flowing even when leaves and debris pile up.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $35.00
> - Unit: Each
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price and unit are present.

## Included
- Clear any debris and confirm the downspout is flowing properly
- Cut or adjust the Gutter Stick to fit your gutter and downspout setup
- Install it securely in the outlet
- Test to make sure it’s seated properly and working as intended

## Not Included
- Full gutter cleaning (must be done first if the gutters are clogged)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Guarantees downspouts don’t get clogged
- Keeps water flowing during heavy rain
- Low-cost, low-maintenance upgrade to protect your home
