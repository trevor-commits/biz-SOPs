# Gutter Ball Installation (Customer Purchased)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll install the wire “gutter balls” you purchased into your downspout outlets to help keep big debris out.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Clear the outlet area and seat each strainer in the downspout opening
- Trim/adjust as needed for a snug fit
- Quick flow check to make sure water can pass

## Not Included
- Full gutter cleaning or repairs
- Supplying the strainers
- Removal of guards or major adjustments to your system

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps stop large leaves/pinecones from entering downspouts
- Simple, no-cost add-on when you supply the parts
