# Downspout End Trim & Safety Cap

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If a downspout end gets bent inward, it can catch debris and slow drainage. We trim off the bent section, then cover the sharp cut edge with a plastic connector/cap.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $30.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Cut off the bent-in/downspout end section to reopen the outlet
- Deburr and smooth the cut edge as much as possible
- Install a plastic connector/cap to cover the sharp edge
- Clean up metal shavings and debris

## Not Included
- Rerouting downspouts or changing discharge location
- Adding extensions, splash blocks, or underground drains
- Repairing crushed downspout sections above the cut
- Gutter cleaning or downspout clearing

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps debris and water exit the downspout more freely
- Removes the sharp-edge contact risk at the cut point
- Keeps the discharge end cleaner and more uniform

Priced per downspout end. Once a downspout is cut shorter, changing the length later requires additional parts/work.
