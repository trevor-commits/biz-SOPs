# Reattach Drip Edge & Secure Gutter – Hanger/Screw Re-Fasten

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If your gutter is sagging or pulling away, we’ll reattach the drip edge where needed and re-secure the gutter to the fascia so it sits tight and drains the way it should.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $50.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Reattach loose drip edge sections at the gutter line
- Re-seat gutter and re-secure to fascia with screws where needed
- Refasten/replace loose gutter hangers (minor hardware as needed)
- Check for proper slope and obvious gaps along the repaired section
- Clean up any debris created during the work

## Not Included
- Full gutter cleaning or downspout clearing
- Sealed seam repairs or leak troubleshooting beyond the worked area
- Replacing damaged fascia/rafters/drip edge over large sections
- Gutter guard install or guard removal
- Exterior gutter face cleaning

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces dripping and overflow behind the gutter
- Helps prevent fascia rot and water staining
- Stops sagging sections from getting worse
- Improves drainage and keeps seams under less stress

Pricing is typically per linear foot (or by the section). If the fascia wood is soft/rotted or the drip edge is damaged, we can’t anchor into it until that’s addressed.
