# Downspout Outlet Repair – Rivet

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If a screw is blocking the downspout outlet, it can catch debris and slow drainage. We remove the obstruction and replace it with a rivet so water can flow through more freely.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Remove the screw or obstruction at the downspout outlet
- Clear the outlet opening at the connection point
- Install a rivet to secure the outlet without blocking flow
- Quick flow check at the outlet area

## Not Included
- Full downspout snaking or clearing beyond the outlet area
- Replacing the outlet, downspout, or gutter section
- Gutter cleaning or roof debris removal
- Leak sealing outside the outlet connection

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces snag points that can trap leaves and grit
- Helps improve drainage speed at the outlet
- Lowers the chance of repeat clogs at that connection

## Just So You Know
Priced per outlet. If the outlet or downspout is crushed, split, or heavily corroded, replacement is a separate service item.
