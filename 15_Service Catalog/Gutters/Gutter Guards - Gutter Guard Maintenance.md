# Gutter Guard Maintenance

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Even with guards installed, debris can still build up on top or get through over time. This service keeps your protected gutter system flowing properly and avoids hidden clogs or overflow issues.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $400.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Remove surface debris sitting on top of the guards
- Open sections (if needed) to check inside for trapped debris or clogs
- Clear out downspouts or trouble areas if buildup is found
- Inspect the guards for damage, sagging, or signs of failure

## Not Included
- Full guard removal (available separately)
- Repair or replacement of broken guards (quoted if needed)
- Full gutter cleaning of unguarded systems

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Keeps your guard system working like it should
- Prevents overflow from debris piling up on top
- Helps spot issues early so you can avoid bigger repairs
- Extends the life of your gutter and guard system

Pricing depends on how much debris has built up and the type of guard installed. If your guards haven’t been checked in a while, this is a smart seasonal service.
