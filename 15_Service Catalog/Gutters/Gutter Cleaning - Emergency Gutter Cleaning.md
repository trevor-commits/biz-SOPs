# Emergency Gutter Cleaning

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If your gutters are overflowing, your downspouts are clogged, or water’s going where it shouldn’t — we’ll get out there quickly to clear it up and stop the damage from getting worse.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Remove leaves, pine needles, and debris causing the blockage
- Clear out downspouts to restore water flow
- Check for clogs, sagging sections, or water spilling over
- Prioritize key problem areas to stop the immediate issue

## Not Included
- Full gutter system inspection (available afterward if needed)
- Repairs or major gutter adjustments
- Non-urgent general cleaning (this service is for active issues)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Prevents water from backing up under shingles or into your home
- Protects your siding, fascia, and foundation during heavy rain
- Gives you fast peace of mind when timing matters
- Can help avoid more expensive damage or repairs

This service is designed for urgent situations only. Pricing reflects priority scheduling, availability, and severity of the issue. If you’re in the middle of a storm or notice immediate overflow — don’t wait to reach out.
