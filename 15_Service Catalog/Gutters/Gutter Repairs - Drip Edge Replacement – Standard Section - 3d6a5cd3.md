# Drip Edge Replacement – Standard Section

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Water can slip behind the gutter when the drip edge is missing or the gutter has sagged. We re-level that stretch and install new drip edge so runoff sheds where it should.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Remove existing fasteners as needed in the work area
- Re-level the gutter along the affected long run (about 10')
- Install new drip edge and secure with appropriate hardware
- Reseat/secure gutter attachment points in that section
- Clean up jobsite debris

## Not Included
- Full gutter cleaning or downspout clearing
- Replacing gutter sections, downspouts, or hangers outside the work area
- Wood rot repair, fascia replacement, or painting fascia/trim
- Roofing repairs beyond the drip edge line

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps reduce overflow behind the gutter at that section
- Improves drainage and reduces standing water in a sag
- Helps protect fascia edges from repeated wetting

## Just So You Know
Priced per large section. If we uncover hidden damage behind the gutter/drip edge, we’ll flag it before doing anything beyond this scope.
