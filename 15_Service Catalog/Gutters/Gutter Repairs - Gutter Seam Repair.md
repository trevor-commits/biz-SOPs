# Gutter Seam Repair

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll reseal leaking gutter seams so water flows through your downspouts instead of dripping onto your home.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $50.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Inspect the leaking joint
- Clean, dry, and reseal with durable, weatherproof sealant
- Check flow once cured

## Not Included
- Full system reseal or replacement
- Downspout repairs or cleaning

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Prevents water damage to siding, fascia, and foundation
- Extends the life of your gutters without full replacement
- Quick, affordable fix

We’ll let you know if the leak needs more than sealant to fix.
