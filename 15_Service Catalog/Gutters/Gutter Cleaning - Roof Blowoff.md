# Roof Blowoff

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll remove loose debris like leaves, pine needles, and branches from your roof to help prevent clogs, buildup, and early wear. It’s a smart seasonal service, especially before or after storms.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $150.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Use a leaf blower to clear loose debris from the roof surface
- Focus on valleys, overhangs, and debris-prone areas
- Clear out any debris that’s gathered around vents or flashing
- Do a quick visual check for any noticeable roof concerns

## Not Included
- Gutter cleaning (can be added separately)
- Moss, algae, or stuck-on debris removal
- Repairs or inspections for leaks or roof structure issues

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps prevent gutter and downspout clogs
- Reduces roof wear from moisture-trapping debris
- Keeps things tidy and lowers the risk of pests and water damage
- Quick, affordable maintenance between full cleanings

We don’t walk steep or brittle roofs unless it’s safe to do so — some roofs may be blown off from a ladder.
Pricing depends on roof size, access, and how much debris needs to be removed.
