# Downspout Clearing

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> If your gutters are fine but the downspouts aren’t draining properly, we can clear out the blockage and get things flowing again. This is a focused service for downspouts only.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $75.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Check each downspout for visible clogs or backup
- Use hand tools, hose flush, or other methods to clear the blockage
- Confirm water flows freely through the full downspout
- Inspect for signs of separation or underground drainage issues

## Not Included
- Full gutter cleaning
- Repairs to damaged or disconnected downspouts
- Clearing underground drain lines (quoted separately if needed)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Stops water from backing up and spilling over the gutters
- Helps protect siding, foundations, and landscaping
- Fast, low-cost fix for isolated flow issues
- Can save you from needing a full gutter cleaning if that’s not the problem

Pricing depends on how many downspouts need clearing and whether the clogs are minor or packed in deep.
