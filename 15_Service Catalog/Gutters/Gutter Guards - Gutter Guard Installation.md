# Gutter Guard Installation

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We install durable metal micro-mesh guards that keep leaves and pine needles out while letting water flow through.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $10.00
> - Unit: Linear Ft.
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price and unit are present.

## Included
- Inspect and measure your existing gutters for fit
- Custom cut and install metal micro-mesh guards
- Secure each section for a tight, clean finish
- Check key spots (especially at downspouts) for proper flow

## Not Included
- Gutter cleaning before install
- Removal of old guards
- Repairs to damaged or sagging sections

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Prevents frequent clogs and overflow
- Helps protect fascia, siding, and foundation
- Low-profile look that blends with the roofline
- Includes your first annual guard maintenance at no extra cost

We recommend yearly guard maintenance to keep everything flowing right. Pricing is based on linear footage, roof height, and access.
