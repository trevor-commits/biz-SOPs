# Solar Panel Cleaning

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Dust, pollen, and especially bird droppings can block sunlight and even cause hot spots that damage your panels over time. We’ll safely clean your solar system so it keeps producing at its best.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $12.00
> - Unit: Each
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price and unit are present.

## Included
- Rinse panels with purified water for a spot-free finish
- Use solar panel–specific brushes to remove stuck-on debris
- Check for obvious cracks, shading issues, or anything unusual on the surface
- Look underneath for signs of nesting or chewed wiring from birds or pests

## Not Included
- Bird proofing (available as a separate service)
- Repairs to damaged panels, wiring, or roofing materials

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Improves efficiency by letting more sunlight through
- Removes bird droppings that can etch glass and shorten panel lifespan
- Helps prevent hot spots and costly long-term damage
- Recommended 1–2 times a year, especially near trees or heavy bird activity

We don’t use pressure washers or harsh chemicals — only solar-approved tools and methods. Pricing depends on system size, layout, and roof access.
