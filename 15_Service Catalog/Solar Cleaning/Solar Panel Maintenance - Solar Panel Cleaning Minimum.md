# Solar Panel Cleaning Minimum

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Dust, pollen, and especially bird droppings can block sunlight and create hot spots that damage panels. We’ll clean your system safely so it keeps producing at its best.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $200.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Rinse panels with purified water
- Gently brush with solar panel–specific tools to remove stuck-on debris
- Light visual check for cracks, shading issues, or anything unusual
- Look underneath for signs of nesting or chewed wiring (where accessible)

## Not Included
- Bird proofing (available separately)
- Repairs to panels, wiring, or roofing materials

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Improves energy production by letting in more sunlight
- Removes bird droppings that can cause hot spots and etch the glass
- Helps prevent long-term damage and keeps your system performing well

We don’t use pressure washers or harsh chemicals — only solar-approved methods. Pricing depends on panel count, roof access, and layout.
