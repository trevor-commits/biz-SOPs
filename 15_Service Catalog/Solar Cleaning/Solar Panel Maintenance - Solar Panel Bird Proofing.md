# Solar Panel Bird Proofing

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Birds love nesting under solar panels, but the droppings and debris they leave behind can cause serious problems — from chewed wires to lower energy output. Even worse, bird waste carries bacteria and parasites that can be harmful to your health. We’ll safely secure your system with protective barriers to keep birds and pests out for good.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Linear Ft.
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Inspect panels and roof edges for nesting activity or damage
- Clean out droppings and debris around the perimeter
- Install a protective mesh barrier around your solar array
- Secure everything without drilling into or damaging your panels

## Not Included
- Full panel cleaning (available separately or bundled)
- Repairs to already damaged wiring or roofing materials
- Removal of active nests during restricted wildlife periods (we’ll advise if that applies)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Stops birds from nesting under your panels and making a mess
- Protects wiring, shingles, and panels from costly damage
- Helps maintain maximum energy production by keeping airflow clear
- Reduces exposure to bird waste, which can be unsafe for your health
- Long-term, low-maintenance solution

## Just So You Know
Pricing depends on system size, roof access, and layout. This service can be paired with solar panel cleaning for the best results.
