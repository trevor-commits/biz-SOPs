# Solar Panel Bird Proofing

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> Birds love nesting under solar panels, but the droppings and debris they leave behind can damage wiring, lower energy output, create fire risks, and spread harmful bacteria. We’ll install a protective barrier to keep them out for good.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $40.00
> - Unit: Each
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price and unit are present.

## Included
- Inspect panels and roof edges for nesting or droppings
- Clean out debris around the perimeter
- Install protective mesh around your solar array
- Secure everything without drilling into or damaging the panels

## Not Included
- Full solar panel cleaning (available separately or bundled)
- Cleaning up bird droppings outside of the solar panel area (available separately as bird waste cleaning)
- Repairs to damaged wiring or roofing materials

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Stops birds from nesting under your panels
- Protects wiring, shingles, and panels from costly damage
- Prevents fire hazards caused by dry nesting material near wiring
- Helps your system produce at its full potential
- Reduces health risks from bird waste buildup

Pricing depends on system size, layout, and roof access.
