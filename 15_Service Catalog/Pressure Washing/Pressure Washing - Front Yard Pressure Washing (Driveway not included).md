# Front Yard Pressure Washing (Driveway not included)

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash the main ground-level surfaces in your front yard to remove dirt, moss, algae, and grime. This is a quick but noticeable way to freshen up your entry area and walkways.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $150.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Pressure wash walkways, porch steps, and other concrete or paved areas
- Apply a cleaning solution to help break down organic buildup
- Rinse thoroughly to remove dirt, moss, and surface stains
- Move lightweight items as needed — if anything is fragile or valuable, we ask that it be moved ahead of time

## Not Included
- Driveway cleaning (can be added separately)
- Oil stain removal — some improvement is likely, but stains may not fully come out
- Cleaning of siding, garage doors, or vertical surfaces

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Instantly improves curb appeal
- Removes slippery moss and buildup from entryways
- Helps preserve your concrete and pavers by clearing off grime

Pricing depends on the total area being cleaned and how dirty the surface is.
