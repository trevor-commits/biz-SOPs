# Full Exterior Ground Pressure Washing – Front, Backyard & Driveway

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash all major ground-level surfaces around your home — including patios, walkways, entryways, and more. This is a full exterior refresh that covers both the front and backyard in one visit.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $500.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Pressure wash walkways, patios, entry areas, and other concrete or paved surfaces
- Apply a cleaning solution to break down dirt, moss, and algae
- Rinse thoroughly to lift buildup and improve surface appearance
- Focus on high-traffic and shaded areas where grime tends to build up
- Move outdoor furniture or items as needed — if something is fragile or valuable, we ask that it be moved ahead of time

## Not Included
- Oil stain removal — they usually improve, but likely won’t come out completely
- Cleaning of siding, garage doors, outdoor furniture, or vertical surfaces
- Pool deck or sealed/decorative surfaces unless discussed ahead of time

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Gives your entire exterior a clean, refreshed look
- Removes slippery moss and algae for better safety
- Helps extend the life of your hardscapes by clearing off grime before it settles in
- Ideal for seasonal maintenance or getting ready for guests

Pricing depends on square footage, surface type, and overall condition.
Includes:
- Application of a cleaning solution to assist with organic growth removal
- Pressure washing of front and backyard patios, walkways, and hard surfaces
- Driveway cleaning (up to sidewalk or curb)
- Rinse-down of surrounding borders and landscaping edges
- Final rinse for a clean and finished look

Benefits:
- Loosens and removes organic growth like moss and algae for safer surfaces
- Instantly improves curb appeal and backyard appearance
- Prepares surfaces for sealing or staining if desired
- Helps preserve the condition of concrete, pavers, and other hardscape materials

Note:
This service does not include oil stain removal, though visible improvement is common. Ask about optional degreasing for targeted stain treatment.
Pricing is based on total square footage and overall site complexity.
