# Backyard Pressure Washing

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash the main surfaces in your backyard to remove dirt, moss, algae, and buildup. Perfect for patios, walkways, and other areas that need a refresh.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $900.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Pressure wash patios, concrete, pavers, and walkways
- Apply a cleaning solution to help break down grime and organic growth
- Rinse thoroughly to flush out dirt and lift buildup
- Focus on high-traffic areas and shaded spots where moss and algae collect
- Move furniture and outdoor items as needed — if something is fragile or valuable, we ask that it be moved ahead of time

## Not Included
- Oil stain removal (available separately if needed)
- Cleaning of vertical walls, furniture, or delicate materials

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Makes your outdoor space look cleaner and more inviting
- Removes slippery buildup for improved safety
- Helps preserve your hard surfaces by keeping grime from settling in

This service is for ground-level surfaces only.
If you have a pool, we’ll do our best to keep debris out — but some may still end up in the water during the cleaning process.
Pricing depends on square footage and surface condition.
