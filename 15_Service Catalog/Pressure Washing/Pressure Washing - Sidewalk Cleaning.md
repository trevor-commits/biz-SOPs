# Sidewalk Cleaning

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash your sidewalk to remove dirt, grime, moss, and buildup that collect over time. A clean sidewalk makes your property look better and safer for anyone passing through.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $100.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Pressure wash the full sidewalk area from end to end
- Apply a cleaning solution to help lift dirt and organic buildup
- Rinse thoroughly to remove grime and improve traction
- Focus on high-traffic or shaded areas where moss tends to grow
- Move small items or planters if needed — if anything is fragile or valuable, we ask that it be moved ahead of time

## Not Included
- Driveway or walkway areas beyond the sidewalk
- Oil stain removal — some improvement is likely, but not guaranteed
- Cleaning of walls, fences, or vertical surfaces

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Instantly improves curb appeal
- Helps prevent slips by removing moss and grime
- Keeps your entry area looking clean and maintained
- Quick, low-cost improvement for homeowners or property managers

Pricing depends on sidewalk length, condition, and buildup.
