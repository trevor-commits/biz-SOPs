# Front Walkway Moss/Algae Treatment & Pressure Washing

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We treat organic growth to slow regrowth, then pressure wash to clean the surface and lift dirt, grime, and buildup.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Apply a moss/algae treatment to target organic growth
- Allow proper dwell time before rinsing
- Pressure wash the walkway to remove surface dirt and buildup
- Rinse edges and clean up loosened debris

## Not Included
- Pressure washing walls, siding, or the house
- Rust removal or specialty stain removal
- Crack repair, patching, or sealing
- Guaranteed removal of deep stains (results vary by surface and age)
- Moving heavy items or clearing stored materials

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces slippery spots caused by organic growth
- Cleans the surface, not just the green growth
- Helps brighten concrete and improve curb appeal
- Slows regrowth compared to pressure washing alone

Priced per square foot. We can improve most organic staining and surface buildup, but some discoloration may remain if it’s set deep into the concrete or pavers.
