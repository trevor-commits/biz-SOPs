# Concrete Stain Removal

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll treat specific spots on your concrete to reduce or completely remove stains like oil, rust, grease, or other stubborn buildup. This is a focused service meant to improve problem areas — and in many cases, the stains may be fully removed.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $100.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Apply targeted stain removers and degreasers based on the type of stain
- Agitate with brushes or tools to help lift it from the surface
- Rinse and re-treat if needed for the best result
- Keep you informed on what to expect depending on the condition and age of the concrete

## Not Included
- Full pressure washing of the surrounding area (can be added separately)
- Guaranteed removal of deeply set-in stains — though we’ll do our best, and in many cases they can be fully removed

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Helps improve the look of your concrete without needing to clean the whole surface
- Makes driveways and walkways look more even and maintained
- Can reduce or remove stains that stand out after pressure washing

This is a great add-on if you’re already getting your driveway or patio cleaned. Pricing depends on how many spots need attention and how deep the stains are.
