# Tile Cleaning

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll gently pressure wash your outdoor tile surfaces to remove dirt, grime, and organic buildup without damaging the tile or grout. Great for patios, porches, and walkways made of ceramic, porcelain, or stone tile.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Apply a cleaning solution to help break down buildup on the tile and in the grout lines
- Gently pressure wash the surface without damaging the finish
- Rinse thoroughly to lift dirt, moss, and algae
- Move outdoor items as needed — if anything is fragile or valuable, we ask that it be moved ahead of time

## Not Included
- Re-grouting or tile repair
- Oil or rust stain removal — some improvement is possible but not guaranteed
- Sealing (available separately if needed)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Restores the natural color and shine of your tile
- Removes slippery moss and grime for safer footing
- Helps prevent long-term damage from buildup getting into grout or joints
- Keeps your patio or porch looking clean and inviting

Pricing depends on tile type, square footage, and surface condition.
