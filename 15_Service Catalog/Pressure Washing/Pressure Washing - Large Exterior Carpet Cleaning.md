# Large Exterior Carpet Cleaning

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll deep clean large outdoor carpeted areas to remove dirt, organic buildup, and stains so the surface looks better and is safer to walk on.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Pre-treat heavy traffic lanes and stains
- Pressure wash using carpet-safe settings
- Apply a light detergent/degreaser as needed
- Rinse thoroughly to reduce residue
- Spot-treat remaining problem areas (as possible)

## Not Included
- Interior carpet cleaning
- Repairs, re-stretching, or re-gluing carpet
- Paint, rust, or permanent dye/bleach stain removal
- Mold remediation or sub-surface moisture correction
- Moving heavy furniture or large built-ins

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Lifts embedded dirt and grime
- Reduces slippery organic growth
- Helps carpet dry cleaner and smell fresher
- Extends the usable life of the carpet

## Just So You Know
Priced per sq ft. Results depend on the material type, the extent of organic growth, and how much chemical is needed, and some stains may lighten but not fully come out.
