# Patio Concrete Cleaning

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash your patio to remove dirt, grime, moss, algae, and years of buildup. Whether it’s concrete, pavers, or another hard surface, we’ll get it looking cleaner and safer.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $100.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Pressure wash the full patio area
- Apply a cleaning solution to break down organic growth and grime
- Rinse thoroughly to lift and clear buildup
- Focus on shaded or high-traffic areas where dirt and moss collect
- Move outdoor items as needed — if something is fragile or valuable, we ask that it be moved ahead of time

## Not Included
- Oil stain removal — they typically improve, but won’t fully disappear
- Cleaning of walls, patio furniture, or decorative items not part of the surface

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Makes your patio look clean and more inviting
- Removes slippery moss and algae
- Helps protect your surface by keeping buildup from setting in

Priced per square foot, the extent of organic growth and how much chemical is needed.
