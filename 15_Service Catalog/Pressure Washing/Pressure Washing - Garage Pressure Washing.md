# Garage Pressure Washing

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash the garage floor to remove dirt and grime so it’s safer and easier to keep clean.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $150.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Sweep/blow out loose debris
- Pre-treat heavy buildup as needed
- Pressure wash the concrete floor surface
- Rinse toward the garage door and tidy up runoff at the threshold

## Not Included
- Walls, shelving, cabinets, or stored items
- Oil/rust stain removal (may improve but not guaranteed)
- Epoxy/coating work or repairs to cracks/joints
- Hauling away large items or trash

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Removes slippery buildup and tracked-in grime
- Cuts down on odors and dust on the floor
- Makes it easier to keep the space clean
