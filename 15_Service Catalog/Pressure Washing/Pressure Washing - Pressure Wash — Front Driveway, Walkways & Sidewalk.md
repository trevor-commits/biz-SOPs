# Pressure Wash — Front Driveway, Walkways & Sidewalk

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash your front driveway, walkways, and sidewalk to remove built-up dirt and organic growth so the surfaces look cleaner and feel safer.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $0.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: CRM stores this as $0.00. Treat as quoted, complimentary, or incomplete until verified.

## Included
- Pre-treat heavy organic growth where needed
- Surface clean driveway, walkways, and sidewalk
- Rinse edges and rinse down surrounding areas
- Blow off remaining water and debris and tidy up

## Not Included
- Washing siding, stucco, or walls
- Oil/rust stain removal (may improve, not guaranteed)
- Sealing or re-sanding joints (pavers)
- Gum removal or specialty stain treatments
- Moving heavy items (planters, furniture, vehicles)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces slick algae/mildew on walking areas
- Improves curb appeal without painting or resurfacing
- Helps slow down future buildup

## Just So You Know
Pricing is typically per sq ft and depends on total area and how heavy the buildup is. Oil stains usually lighten but may not fully come out, and surfaces can look uneven until fully dry.
