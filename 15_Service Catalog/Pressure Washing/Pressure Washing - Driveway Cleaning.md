# Driveway Cleaning

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash your entire driveway to remove dirt, grime, moss, and buildup. Whether your driveway is small, wide, long, or curved, we’ll get it looking fresh again.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $200.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Pressure wash the full driveway from edge to edge
- Apply a cleaning solution to break down dirt and organic buildup
- Rinse thoroughly to lift away surface grime
- Focus on heavy-use areas and common trouble spots
- Move lightweight items as needed — if anything is fragile or valuable, we ask that it be moved ahead of time

## Not Included
- Oil stain removal — they won’t fully come out, but they usually look noticeably better
- Cleaning of sidewalks, garage doors, walls, or any areas not part of the driveway

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Makes a huge difference in curb appeal
- Removes slippery moss and algae for better traction
- Helps preserve the life of your driveway by keeping grime from settling in

## Just So You Know
Pricing depends on total square footage and surface condition.
