# Paver Pressure Washing

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll pressure wash your paver surfaces to remove dirt, moss, algae, and grime that build up over time. Whether it’s a patio, walkway, or driveway made of pavers, we’ll clean them without damaging the joints or lifting the sand.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $100.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: Yes
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Pressure wash the surface of the pavers to remove dirt and organic buildup
- Apply a cleaning solution to help loosen grime and stains
- Rinse thoroughly, paying attention to joints and edges
- Focus on high-traffic or shaded areas where moss and algae collect
- Move furniture or items as needed — if anything is fragile or valuable, we ask that it be moved ahead of time

## Not Included
- Re-sanding or sealing (available separately)
- Oil stain removal — some improvement is likely, but not guaranteed
- Deep restoration for sunken, heavily stained, or damaged pavers

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Restores the natural look and color of your pavers
- Removes slippery buildup for safer walking areas
- Helps prevent long-term wear and keeps the surface looking maintained
- Great prep if you plan to re-sand or seal afterward

## Just So You Know
Pricing depends on square footage, buildup level, and layout complexity.
