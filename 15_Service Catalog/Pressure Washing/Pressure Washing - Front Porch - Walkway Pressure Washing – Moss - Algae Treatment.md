# Front Porch/Walkway Pressure Washing – Moss/Algae Treatment

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We’ll clean your front porch surfaces and apply a moss/algae treatment to slow regrowth in damp or shaded areas.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $100.00
> - Unit: Quoted / not specified
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price is present; confirm the pricing basis before using it externally.

## Included
- Clear loose debris from the porch area
- Pre-treat moss/algae buildup where needed
- Pressure wash flat surfaces and steps/walkways in the porch zone
- Rinse down surrounding edges and tidy up the work area
- Apply a moss/algae treatment to help reduce future growth

## Not Included
- Washing siding or walls (flatwork only)
- Sealing or coating the surface
- Deep stain removal (rust, paint, or heavy oil)
- Repairs to concrete, pavers, or mortar joints
- Movement of fragile items

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Reduces slippery green buildup on walking surfaces
- Helps slow regrowth in problem spots
- Makes regular upkeep easier between cleanings

Pricing is per square foot. Treatment helps slow new growth, but regrowth can still happen depending on shade, moisture, and surface type.
