# Driveway Moss Treatment & Pressure Washing

> [!summary] Quick Summary
> We treat moss/algae to slow regrowth, then pressure wash to thoroughly clean the driveway surface and lift dirt, grime, and buildup.

> [!info] Pricing
> - CRM price: $200.00
> - Unit: 1
> - Taxable: No
> - Online booking enabled: No
> - Pricing note: Fixed CRM price and unit are present.

## Included
- Apply a moss/algae treatment to target organic growth
- Allow proper dwell time before rinsing
- Pressure wash the driveway to remove surface dirt and buildup
- Rinse down edges and clean up loosened debris

## Not Included
- Pressure washing walls, siding, or the house
- Rust removal or specialty stain removal
- Crack repair, patching, or sealing
- Guaranteed oil stain removal (often improves, but results vary)
- Removing heavy items (planters, vehicles, stored materials)

## Best Fit / When To Offer
- Cleans the driveway surface, not just the moss
- Reduces slippery areas caused by organic growth
- Helps brighten concrete and improve curb appeal
- Slows regrowth compared to pressure washing alone

Priced per square foot. We can improve most buildup and organic staining, but some deep stains may remain depending on the surface condition and age.
