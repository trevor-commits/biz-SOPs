> [!note]- Source Copy
> If you're not sure how many windows you have, how dirty they really are, or what it’ll cost to get them cleaned — we’ll come take a look and give you a no-pressure quote. In many cases, we can take care of the job on the spot if the timing and scope work out.
>
> What we do:
> • Walk around the property and count all cleanable windows
> • Check the condition of the glass, height, and access points
> • Ask if you’d like interior, exterior, or both
> • Recommend any add-ons (like screens, tracks, mirrors, or skylights)
> • Provide a clear quote based on what you need
> • Same-day cleaning may be available depending on time and prep
>
> Why it’s worth it:
> • Gives you a firm quote before committing to anything
> • Saves you a second visit if we’re able to do the work right away
> • We’ll show you options without upselling what you don’t need
> • Great way to customize your cleaning based on your goals and budget
>
> What’s not included:
> • Cleaning itself (unless scheduled or approved at the time of the estimate)
> • Full walkthrough of interiors unless requested in advance
> • Repairs, hard water removal, or specialty services not discussed during the visit
>
> Just so you know:
> Estimates are free, and we’re happy to adjust them on the spot if you want to add or remove anything. You can always ask questions or make changes before we start.
