> [!note]- Source Copy
> If something we cleaned or installed isn’t holding up the way it should, we’ll come back out and make it right at no charge.
>
> What we do:
> • Review the original service notes and your concern
> • Re-clean or re-check the specific area related to the original visit
> • Touch up missed spots within the original scope
> • Confirm drainage/flow or performance before we leave
>
> Why it’s worth it:
> • Saves you from paying twice for the same issue
> • Gets the result back to the original standard
> • Keeps small problems from turning into a bigger service call
>
> What’s not included:
> • New areas that weren’t part of the original service
> • Repairs, replacements, or upgrades
> • Exterior faces of gutters
> • Full window cleaning if windows were splashed during gutter work
>
> Just so you know:
> This is a per-visit return service at no charge when it’s tied to the original scope. It doesn’t cover new issues, add-ons, or unrelated work discovered after the first appointment.
