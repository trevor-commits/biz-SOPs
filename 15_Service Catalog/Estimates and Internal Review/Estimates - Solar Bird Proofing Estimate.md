> [!note]- Source Copy
> If you're seeing signs of nesting, droppings, or debris under your solar panels — or just want to prevent a problem before it starts — we’ll come take a look and give you a quote for bird proofing. In some cases, we may be able to install same-day if everything lines up.
>
> What we do:
> • Inspect the perimeter and underside of your solar panels
> • Check for signs of nesting, droppings, or chewed wiring
> • Measure the system layout to determine materials and access needs
> • Explain how bird proofing works and what your options are
> • Provide a clear quote based on panel count, roof type, and access
> • Same-day installation may be available depending on timing and materials
>
> Why it’s worth it:
> • Prevents costly damage to wires, panels, and roofing
> • Stops birds and pests from nesting or making a mess under your system
> • Helps protect your energy production and avoid repairs
> • Gives you a long-term solution with minimal visual impact
>
> What’s not included:
> • Panel cleaning (available separately or as part of an install package)
> • Removal of active nests (if found, we’ll advise based on timing and legal restrictions)
> • Repairs to damaged wiring or roofing materials
>
> Just so you know:
> Estimates are always free. If you’re also due for a solar panel cleaning, we can bundle both services into one visit — just let us know ahead of time.
