>
> What we do:
> • Walk the property with you (or independently, if preferred)
> • Inspect the area(s) of concern and explain available service options
> • Offer add-on suggestions if we see anything worth flagging
> • Provide a quote — either on-site or shortly after
> • Perform same-day service if time, prep, and materials allow
>
> Why it’s worth it:
> • Gives you a clear understanding of what needs to be done
> • Saves you a second appointment if we’re able to take care of things right then
> • Honest, no-pressure recommendations based on what we see
> • Great option for seasonal maintenance or property prep
>
> What’s not included:
> • Full written reports for real estate or insurance purposes
> • Interior walkthroughs unless scheduled in advance
> • Guaranteed same-day service — depends on scope and prep
>
> Just so you know:
> Estimates are always free and based on the real condition of your property. If we’ve worked together before, we’ll refer back to your service history when recommending anything new.
